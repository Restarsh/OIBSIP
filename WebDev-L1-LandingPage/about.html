Our Story

From a dorm room dream to ethically sourced coffee.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020 by college roommates Maya and Diego, BrewBloom started with a simple belief: great coffee shouldn't come at the expense of people or the planet. After a semester abroad visiting coffee farms in Ethiopia, they returned home determined to build a business that values transparency, sustainability, and exceptional flavor.

Today, we work directly with cooperatives in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair wages and environmentally friendly practices. Every bean is roasted in small batches in our roastery in Portland, Oregon, and packaged in 100% compostable bags.

Our commitment goes beyond the cup—we invest a portion of profits into community projects at origin, supporting education and healthcare for farming families.

Our Values

Sustainability

From farm to cup, we minimize environmental impact through organic farming, carbon-neutral shipping, and compostable packaging.

Fair Trade

We pay above-market premiums to ensure farmers can invest in their communities and improve quality of life.

Quality

Small-batch roasting and rigorous cupping ensure every bag meets our exacting flavor standards.
